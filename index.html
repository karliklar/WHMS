Welcome to Woodland Hills Medical Supply


We are a Medical Supply committed to developing custom medications for a wide variety of conditions. We specialize in the following:

    ✓ Sinus problems
    ✓ Biotoxin Illness, including mold
    ✓ Dental applications
    ✓ Dermatological (aesthetics) treatment

We use state-of-the-art technology and FDA-approved chemicals in accordance with the strictest and safest standards, so you can trust that your patients will receive their prescriptions to your exact specifications.
We also provide a wide variety of other compound prescriptions, including for the following:

  • ENT
  • Gastroenterology
  • Rheumatic Treatment
  • Hormone Replacement Therapy
  • and many more!
