Insurance Billing

Woodland Hills Medical Supply has many options when it comes to billing eligible items to either your major medical Insurance carrier or Medicare; or to help you get the right mobility and medical equipment at the lowest possible price.

Medicare:

Woodland Hills Medical Supply is happy to assist you in obtaining reimbursement from Medicare for the following list of eligible products: Lift chairs, Group 3 Power chairs, Patient Lifts, Commodes, Bracing and Orthopedic Splints, Manual Tilt as well as Ultra-light wheelchairs.

Major Insurance:

Woodland Hills Medical Supply is happy to assist you in obtaining reimbursement from many major Insurance carriers for a wide variety of products, including Power and Manual chairs, Walk Aids and much more. Please call us for details.
